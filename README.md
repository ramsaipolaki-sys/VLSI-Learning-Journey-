## What I Learned Today

### What is VLSI?

VLSI (Very Large Scale Integration) is the technology of integrating millions or billions of transistors onto a single chip.

Examples:
- Mobile Processors
- AI Chips
- Memory Chips
- Microcontrollers

- Strong career opportunities in semiconductors
